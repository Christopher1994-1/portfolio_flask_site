About This Site

I developed a website using the Python Django framework, it offers a user experience with the ability to capture customer information such as emails and phone numbers, and store them securely in a MySQL database. This enables the site to send targeted and personalized notifications to customers regarding sales, special offers, and friendly reminders to visit the store and take advantage of weekend deals. Furthermore, the site can be equipped with advanced database management capabilities, allowing customers to easily browse and discover the wide range of products. With these features, the site is good for efficiency and convenience.
