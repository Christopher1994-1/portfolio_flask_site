About Us



Nestled in the heart of the Pacific Northwest, Washington State is a place of unparalleled natural beauty. From towering evergreens to majestic mountain ranges, the state's landscapes have captivated artists and photographers for generations. However, for those with a discerning eye, there is another side to Washington that is often overlooked - a moody, almost haunting quality that permeates the grey skies and misty forests.


It is this eerie greyness that serves as the inspiration for my photography website. While some may view the bleakness of Washington as depressing or morbid, I believe that there is a unique beauty to be found in its gloomy landscapes. My photographs aim to capture the essence of this beauty, showcasing the quiet power of the state's overcast skies, rugged coastlines, and brooding forests. Through a combination of stark black and white imagery and muted tones, I hope to transport viewers to a world where the line between beauty and melancholy is blurred, and the ordinary becomes extraordinary. So come, explore the grey depths of Washington with me, and discover the hidden beauty that lies within.
