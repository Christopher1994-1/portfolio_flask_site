About This Site

I have designed and implemented a web platform utilizing the Python-based web framework Flask, which serves as a comprehensive resource for potential investors. The platform features detailed information about the company, including compelling reasons for investment and upcoming events such as shareholder meetings. Additionally, the platform integrates a real-time stock ticker that is updated via a secure API. The platform also as the ability to leverage the power of MySQL databases to store and manage event information and other relevant data, ensuring that investors have access to the most up-to-date information at all times.
